About Us

{{ app_name }} is a website for uploading photos, GIFs, and looping videos under 10 seconds. You can use multiple hashtags and search within captions. You can use the search page to show specific types of content. Users have pages where you can view their uploaded content, their likes, and the uploads which they've commented on. Search for a @username to be redirected to the user's uploaded content gallery. Type a #hashtag into the search bar, and you'll be able to use the Browse menu to subscribe to uploads that have the tag.

The site is currently in its open alpha stage. If you have any suggestions for improving the site, send me a message through the feedback page and I'll be sure to read it. Currently, the site doesn't support cropping photos, clipping videos down to the maximum length, or uploading from a URL, and I'll be working on all of those features. Together, we'll make the site as fun to use as possible before the official launch.
